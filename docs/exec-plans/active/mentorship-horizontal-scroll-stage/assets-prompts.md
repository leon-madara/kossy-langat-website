# Assets and Prompts - Mentorship Horizontal Scroll Stage

## Shared Art Direction

All five mentorship images should feel like they belong to the same editorial system.

Required qualities:
- editorial realism
- serious women-in-engineering tone
- premium but credible, not glossy stock-photo energy
- restrained cinematic lighting
- consistent grading and contrast
- architectural, construction, or technical context when appropriate
- strong composition that supports overlaid typography

## Shared Prompt Template

```text
Create a premium editorial image for a high-end structural engineering mentorship website.

Subject: [INSERT PANEL-SPECIFIC SUBJECT]
Scene: [INSERT PANEL-SPECIFIC SCENE]
Mood: disciplined, intelligent, focused, credible, high-trust
Style: editorial realism, architectural photography influence, clean composition, restrained cinematic lighting, subtle texture, not glossy stock photography
Composition: allow clear negative space for overlaid title and body copy, strong structural lines, visually calm but authoritative
Color direction: neutral premium tones with natural materials, muted steel, concrete, paper, warm daylight or controlled indoor light
Camera feel: realistic lens, professional composition, premium magazine feature image
Important: portray women in engineering with seriousness, competence, and authenticity; avoid exaggerated fashion styling, cliches, or generic corporate stock-photo poses
```
